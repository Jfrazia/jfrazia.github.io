About Me

Hardworking senior of Worcester Polytechnic Institute's (WPI) Interactive Media & Game Development major, specializing in audio engineering and game design. WPI's focus and dedication on to project based learning curriculum has equipped with the knowledge, work ethic and team skills needed to be part of an efficient team.The curriculum has also put my leadership skills to the test, requiring me to step up to the plate to produce products with up to class sized teams.
